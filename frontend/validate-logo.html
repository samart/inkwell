Validation Checklist:

  • Nib Shape: Fountain pen style with sharp shoulders and pentagonal body
  • Nib Details: Center slit from tip to breather hole, circular breather hole
  • Nib Split: Left side bright cyan/blue, right side dark/black
  • Band: Rectangular with rounded corners, neon green outline, vertical separators
  • Circuits: 4 paths with specific routing and circular nodes at endpoints
  • Leaf: Pixelated grid pattern, scattered edges, diagonal stem line
  • Glow Effect: Neon glow on all cyan and green elements
